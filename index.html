01 How does PetPal personalize recommendations for my pet?
PetPal uses AI to analyze your pet’s breed, age, activity level, and dietary preferences. Based on this data, we provide customized meal plans, activity suggestions, and health tips.
02 Is PetPal free to use?
Yes! We offer a free plan with essential pet care recommendations. For advanced features like AI-driven insights and expert consultations, you can upgrade to PetPal Premium, which comes with a 1-month free trial.
03 Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your PetPal Premium subscription anytime—no hidden fees, no hassle. Your pet’s profile and basic features will still be available.
04 Does PetPal work for all types of pets?
Currently, PetPal is optimized for dogs and cats, but we’re working on expanding to support other pets like rabbits, birds, and reptiles! Stay tuned.
05 How does PetPal help with pet health tracking?
PetPal sends customized reminders for vet visits, vaccinations, and wellness check-ups. Plus, you can log your pet’s health milestones to track their progress over time.
